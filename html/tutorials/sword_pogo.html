New items

When doing a down attack with Sword on an enemy, you will get a pogo jump which gives you a bit of height. Since you are hitting an enemy with your sword, this also gives you your aerial movement back. If you do a second down attack before hitting the ground, you will not get a second pogo. ![](https://i.giphy.com/media/eniytiTrIMMYryNijx/giphy.webp)
